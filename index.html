B. Pharm. Project

Cross-sectional study on Hypothyroidism Patients


Knowledge, Understanding, and Quality of Life about Hypothyroidism among Patients of Khulna District in Bangladesh.

This study aimed to assess the knowledge, understanding, and quality of life related to hypothyroidism among patients of Khulna District, Bangladesh. Using a cross-sectional approach, it evaluated patients’ awareness of the disease, understanding of treatment and management, and the impact of hypothyroidism on physical, psychological, and social well-being. The findings are intended to identify knowledge gaps and support improved patient education and care strategies.
